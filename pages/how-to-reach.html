By Air

  • Shimla Airport (Jubbarhatti) — 23 km from city centre
  • Flights from Delhi (Alliance Air, Pawan Hans)
  • Nearest major airport: Chandigarh (115 km away)
  • Helicopter service from Chandigarh also available
  • Taxi/cab from airport: ₹400–600
🚂

By Train

  • UNESCO Heritage Kalka–Shimla Toy Train
  • Departs from Kalka station multiple times daily
  • Journey: ~5 hours through 102 tunnels & 864 bridges
  • Connect to Kalka from Delhi (Shatabdi: 4 hrs)
  • Trains: Shivalik Express, Rail Car, Himalayan Queen
🚌

By Bus

  • HRTC buses from Delhi (ISBT Kashmiri Gate)
  • Volvo AC buses: Delhi to Shimla overnight
  • Journey from Delhi: ~10–12 hours
  • Local HRTC buses from Chandigarh every hour
  • Bus Stand: Shimla ISBT, Tutikandi
🚗

By Car / Road

  • Delhi → Shimla via NH-44 and NH-5: ~345 km
  • Chandigarh → Shimla via NH-5: ~115 km
  • Well-maintained roads with scenic mountain views
  • Drive time: Delhi (~6–7 hrs), Chandigarh (~3 hrs)
  • Note: Private vehicles restricted on Mall Road

Within Shimla

Local Transport in Shimla

🚡

Cable Car / Ropeway

The Jakhu Ropeway connects the Ridge to Jakhu Temple. ₹250 return trip. Best way to avoid the steep climb.

🚕

Local Taxis

Easily available at Shimla bus stand and major points. Fixed fare: ₹20–50 within city. Hire full-day for ₹1,500–2,500.

🚶

On Foot

Mall Road and Ridge area is pedestrian-only. Walking is the best way to explore the town centre and bazaars.

🛺

Auto Rickshaws

Available in lower Shimla areas and for routes below Mall Road. Affordable and convenient for short trips.

ℹ️ Important Note for Road Travellers

Private vehicles are NOT allowed on Mall Road and The Ridge area. You must park your vehicle at designated parking areas (Khalini, Dhalli, or New ISBT) and use local transport or walk to the main tourist areas. The town has a strict no-vehicle zone policy to manage traffic congestion.
