🗓️ Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1 Arrival in Shimla — Settle & Explore Mall Road
  • 🚗 Pickup from Chandigarh / Delhi (as per package)
  • 🏨 Check-in to hotel, freshen up
  • 🚶 Evening stroll on Mall Road & The Ridge
  • 🍽️ Welcome dinner at a local restaurant
  • 🌙 Overnight stay in Shimla
Day 2 Jakhu Temple + Christ Church + Scandal Point
  • 🌅 Early morning trek to Jakhu Temple (2,455m)
  • 🐒 See Hanuman statue & playful monkeys
  • ⛪ Visit Christ Church — Gothic architecture
  • 📸 Scandal Point & Gaiety Theatre
  • 🛍️ Shopping at Lakkar Bazaar
  • 🌙 Overnight stay in Shimla
Day 3 Kufri Day Trip — Snow & Adventure
  • 🚗 Drive to Kufri (16 km from Shimla)
  • 🛷 Enjoy snow activities (seasonal)
  • 🐴 Horse riding at Kufri Fun World
  • 🍎 Visit apple orchards at Fagu
  • 🌄 Sunset views from Chail viewpoint
  • 🌙 Return to Shimla, overnight stay
Day 4 Viceregal Lodge + Toy Train + Chadwick Falls
  • 🏛️ Morning visit to Viceregal Lodge (IIAS)
  • 🚂 Iconic Toy Train ride (Shimla–Barog section)
  • 💧 Chadwick Falls — 67m waterfall
  • 🌲 Glen Forest walk
  • 🍽️ Dinner at rooftop restaurant with mountain views
  • 🌙 Overnight stay in Shimla
Day 5 Departure — Farewell Shimla
  • 🌅 Morning at leisure, last views of the hills
  • 🛍️ Last-minute shopping — woolens, handicrafts
  • 🚗 Drop to Chandigarh / Delhi
  • 👋 Tour ends with beautiful memories
