About Kashmir

Welcome to Paradise on Earth

Jammu & Kashmir is a land of breathtaking beauty, where snow-capped mountains meet serene valleys, pristine lakes reflect the sky, and ancient temples stand as testaments to spiritual heritage. From the iconic Dal Lake to the adventure paradise of Gulmarg, Kashmir offers an unforgettable experience for every traveler.

🛶

Dal Lake

Experience the magic of staying in traditional houseboats and gliding through serene waters on shikaras.

⛷️

Gulmarg Skiing

Asia's premier skiing destination with the world's second-highest gondola ride offering stunning views.

🌸

Mughal Gardens

Explore the magnificent Shalimar Bagh, Nishat Bagh, and Chashme Shahi gardens built by Mughal emperors.

🙏

Vaishno Devi

One of India's most sacred pilgrimage sites, attracting millions of devotees every year.

🏔️

Pahalgam Valley

Gateway to the Amarnath Yatra and a paradise for trekkers, nature lovers, and adventure seekers.

🍎

Saffron & Apples

Famous for the world's finest saffron and delicious apples grown in the fertile Kashmir valley.

Must Visit

Top Attractions in Kashmir

From serene Dal Lake to the meadows of Gulmarg — a mix of nature, culture and adventure.

Lake ⭐ 4.9

Dal Lake (Srinagar)

Renowned for houseboats and shikara rides; a peaceful experience with Mughal gardens nearby. The jewel of Srinagar offers stunning sunrise and sunset views.

📍 Srinagar 🕐 Half to Full Day 🆓 Free Entry
Meadow ⭐ 4.8

Gulmarg

Famous for skiing, gondola rides, and alpine meadows — a year-round destination for adventure and views. Home to Asia's highest cable car.

📍 52 km from Srinagar 🕐 Full Day 💰 Gondola ₹750+
Valley ⭐ 4.7

Pahalgam

A scenic valley ideal for trekking, river rafting and as a base for the Amarnath Yatra. Surrounded by pine forests and the Lidder River.

📍 95 km from Srinagar 🕐 Full Day 💰 Entry ₹50
Meadow ⭐ 4.6

Sonamarg

The 'Meadow of Gold' offers stunning glacier views, trekking routes, and the gateway to Ladakh via Zoji La Pass.

📍 80 km from Srinagar 🕐 Full Day 🐴 Horse Riding Available
Temple ⭐ 4.9

Vaishno Devi Temple

One of India's holiest Hindu temples, located in the Trikuta Mountains. A 13 km trek from Katra offers a spiritual journey.

📍 Katra, Jammu 🕐 6-8 hours trek 🆓 Free Entry
Garden ⭐ 4.5

Shalimar Bagh

A magnificent Mughal garden built by Emperor Jahangir for his wife. Features terraced lawns, fountains, and stunning architecture.

📍 Srinagar 🕐 1-2 hours 💰 Entry ₹25
