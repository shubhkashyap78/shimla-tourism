Valleys & Lakes

Highlights

From serene Dal Lake to the meadows of Gulmarg — a mix of nature, culture and adventure.

Lake

Dal Lake (Srinagar)

Renowned for houseboats and shikara rides; a peaceful experience with Mughal gardens nearby.

  • 📍 Srinagar
  • 🕐 Best Time: Apr–Oct
  • 🏃 Duration: Half day
Meadow

Gulmarg

Famous for skiing, gondola rides, and alpine meadows — a year-round destination for adventure and views.

Valley

Pahalgam

A scenic valley ideal for trekking, river rafting and as a base for the Amarnath Yatra.
