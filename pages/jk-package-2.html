🗓️ Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1 Arrival Jammu — City Tour
  • 🚗 Pickup from Jammu Railway Station / Airport
  • 🏨 Check-in hotel, freshen up
  • 🛕 Raghunath Temple — largest temple complex in North India
  • 🏰 Bahu Fort & Bahu Mata Temple
  • 🌊 Mansar Lake — evening boat ride
  • 🌙 Overnight Jammu
Day 2 Vaishno Devi Yatra — Sacred Pilgrimage
  • 🌅 Early morning drive to Katra (50 km)
  • 📋 Yatra slip registration at Katra base camp
  • 🚶 Trek to Vaishno Devi shrine (14 km trek or helicopter)
  • 🙏 Darshan at the holy cave shrine
  • 🔔 Bhairon Baba Temple visit
  • 🌙 Return to Katra, overnight stay
Day 3 Katra → Patnitop — Hill Station
  • 🌅 Morning puja at Katra temple
  • 🚗 Drive to Patnitop (2,024m altitude)
  • 🌿 Sanasar Meadows — paragliding & nature walk
  • 🌲 Nathatop viewpoint — panoramic Himalayan views
  • 🏨 Check-in Patnitop hotel
  • 🌙 Overnight Patnitop
Day 4 Patnitop → Jammu — Surinsar Lake & Temples
  • 🌅 Morning walk in Patnitop forest
  • 🚗 Drive back to Jammu
  • 🌊 Surinsar Lake — twin lake of Mansar
  • 🛕 Peer Kho Cave Temple
  • 🛍️ Jammu bazaar — local shopping
  • 🌙 Overnight Jammu
Day 5 Departure from Jammu
  • 🌅 Morning at leisure
  • 🛕 Optional visit to Ranbireshwar Temple
  • 🚗 Drop to Jammu Railway Station / Airport
  • 👋 Tour ends with divine blessings
