📋 Package Overview

An extraordinary journey through two of India's most diverse states — spot the one-horned rhino at Kaziranga, cruise on the mighty Brahmaputra, and marvel at the ancient Tawang Monastery perched at 10,000 ft. A perfect mix of wildlife, culture, and adventure.

🗓️ Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1 Arrival Guwahati — Kamakhya & Brahmaputra
  • ✈️ Arrive Guwahati Airport, meet & greet
  • 🏯 Kamakhya Temple — one of 51 Shakti Peethas
  • 🌊 Brahmaputra River cruise (sunset)
  • 🏨 Check-in Guwahati hotel
  • 🌙 Overnight Guwahati
Day 2 Kaziranga National Park — Jeep & Elephant Safari
  • 🚗 Drive to Kaziranga (220 km, ~5 hrs)
  • 🏨 Check-in jungle resort
  • 🐘 Evening elephant safari (Central Range)
  • 🌙 Overnight Kaziranga jungle resort
Day 3 Kaziranga Jeep Safari + Tea Garden
  • 🌅 Early morning jeep safari — spot rhinos, elephants, tigers
  • 🦏 Western Range safari (afternoon)
  • 🍵 Visit Assam tea garden — tea tasting
  • 🌙 Overnight Kaziranga
Day 4 Kaziranga → Tawang (Arunachal Pradesh)
  • 🚗 Scenic drive to Tezpur (90 km)
  • 🏯 Agnigarh Hill — Tezpur viewpoint
  • 🚗 Continue to Bomdila (180 km)
  • 🛕 Bomdila Monastery visit
  • 🌙 Overnight Bomdila
Day 5 Sela Pass → Tawang Monastery
  • ❄️ Sela Pass (13,700 ft) — snow & Sela Lake
  • 🕊️ Jaswant Garh War Memorial
  • 🛕 Tawang Monastery — 400-year-old, largest in India
  • 🌄 Tawang War Memorial
  • 🌙 Overnight Tawang
Day 6 Tawang → Guwahati → Departure
  • 🌅 Sunrise at Tawang viewpoint
  • 🚗 Drive to Guwahati (via Bomdila)
  • ✈️ Departure flight from Guwahati
  • 👋 Tour ends — Northeast forever in your heart
