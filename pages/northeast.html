About Northeast

Discover India's Hidden Gem

Northeast India, comprising the Seven Sisters and Sikkim, is a land of unparalleled natural beauty, rich biodiversity, and vibrant tribal cultures. From the living root bridges of Meghalaya to the rhinos of Kaziranga, from the monasteries of Sikkim to the pristine valleys of Arunachal Pradesh — this region offers experiences found nowhere else in India.

🌉

Living Root Bridges

Marvel at the ancient bio-engineering of Meghalaya's living root bridges, grown over centuries by the Khasi tribes.

🦏

Wildlife Sanctuaries

Home to one-horned rhinos, red pandas, snow leopards, and over 800 species of birds across pristine national parks.

🏞️

Cleanest Villages

Visit Mawlynnong, Asia's cleanest village, and experience the eco-friendly lifestyle of Northeast communities.

🛕

Buddhist Monasteries

Explore ancient monasteries in Sikkim, Arunachal Pradesh, and Tawang with stunning Himalayan backdrops.

🍂

Tea Gardens

Walk through endless tea estates in Assam, producing some of the world's finest black tea varieties.

🎭

Tribal Culture

Experience the rich traditions, festivals, and handicrafts of over 200 indigenous tribes across the region.

Top Attractions in Northeast

Home to living root bridges, dense forests, national parks, and rich tribal cultures.

Nature ⭐ 4.9

Living Root Bridges (Meghalaya)

Walk across ancient living root bridges near Cherrapunji and explore misty hills, waterfalls, and caves. A unique bio-engineering marvel.

📍 Cherrapunji, Meghalaya 🕐 Full Day Trek 🆓 Free Entry
Wildlife ⭐ 4.8

Kaziranga National Park

World-famous UNESCO site for one-horned rhinoceros and rich biodiversity — ideal for elephant safaris and birdwatching.

📍 Assam 🕐 Full Day Safari 💰 Safari ₹1,500+
Culture ⭐ 4.9

Tawang Monastery

India's largest monastery at 10,000 ft with Tibetan Buddhist culture and spectacular mountain scenery. A spiritual haven.

📍 Arunachal Pradesh 🕐 Half Day 🆓 Free Entry
City ⭐ 4.7

Gangtok (Sikkim)

Capital city offering stunning Kanchenjunga views, monasteries, ropeway rides, and vibrant MG Marg for shopping and dining.

📍 Sikkim 🕐 2-3 Days 🚡 Ropeway ₹100
Hill Station ⭐ 4.6

Shillong

The 'Scotland of the East' with rolling hills, waterfalls, lakes, and a vibrant music scene. Perfect for nature lovers.

📍 Meghalaya 🕐 2-3 Days 🎸 Live Music Hub
Village ⭐ 4.8

Mawlynnong Village

Asia's cleanest village with bamboo houses, sky walks, and living root bridges. Experience sustainable community living.

📍 Meghalaya 🕐 Half Day 🆓 Free Entry
