🗓️ Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1 Arrival Shimla — Mall Road & The Ridge
  • 🚗 Pickup from Chandigarh railway station / airport
  • 🏨 Check-in hotel, freshen up
  • 🚶 Evening walk on Mall Road & The Ridge
  • 🍽️ Welcome dinner
  • 🌙 Overnight Shimla
Day 2 Shimla Local — Jakhu, Kufri, Toy Train
  • 🌅 Jakhu Temple morning visit
  • 🚂 Toy Train ride experience
  • 🛷 Kufri snow activities (seasonal)
  • 🏛️ Viceregal Lodge visit
  • 🌙 Overnight Shimla
Day 3 Shimla → Kullu (230 km)
  • 🚗 Scenic drive through Narkanda & Rampur
  • 🌊 Beas River views en route
  • 🛕 Bijli Mahadev Temple visit
  • 🏨 Check-in Kullu hotel
  • 🌙 Overnight Kullu
Day 4 Kullu → Manali — River Rafting
  • 🌊 Kullu River Rafting on Beas (Grade 3–4)
  • 🚗 Drive to Manali (40 km)
  • 🏨 Check-in Manali hotel
  • 🛕 Hadimba Devi Temple evening visit
  • 🌙 Overnight Manali
Day 5 Rohtang Pass / Solang Valley
  • ❄️ Rohtang Pass (3,978m) — snow & views (permit required)
  • 🌸 Solang Valley — paragliding, zorbing, skiing
  • 📸 Beas Kund viewpoint
  • 🌙 Overnight Manali
Day 6 Manali Local — Old Manali, Vashisht, Mall Road
  • 🏘️ Old Manali village walk
  • ♨️ Vashisht hot springs & temple
  • 🛍️ Manali Mall Road shopping
  • 🍽️ Farewell dinner
  • 🌙 Overnight Manali
Day 7 Departure from Manali
  • 🌅 Morning at leisure
  • 🚗 Drop to Chandigarh / Delhi (overnight drive)
  • 👋 Tour ends with lifelong memories
