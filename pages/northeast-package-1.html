Discover the untouched beauty of Northeast India — from the wettest place on Earth in Meghalaya to the mystical monasteries of Sikkim. This 7-day journey covers living root bridges, crystal-clear rivers, Kanchenjunga views, and vibrant tribal culture.

🗓️ Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1 Arrival Guwahati → Shillong
  • ✈️ Arrive Guwahati Airport, meet & greet
  • 🚗 Drive to Shillong (100 km, ~3 hrs)
  • 🌊 Umiam Lake (Barapani) — boat ride
  • 🏨 Check-in Shillong hotel
  • 🛍️ Police Bazaar evening walk
  • 🌙 Overnight Shillong
Day 2 Cherrapunji — Waterfalls & Caves
  • 🚗 Drive to Cherrapunji (54 km)
  • 💧 Nohkalikai Falls — India's tallest plunge waterfall
  • 🕳️ Mawsmai Cave — limestone cave exploration
  • 🌊 Seven Sisters Falls viewpoint
  • 🌿 Eco Park & Bangladesh border viewpoint
  • 🌙 Return Shillong, overnight
Day 3 Living Root Bridges & Dawki Crystal River
  • 🌉 Trek to Double Decker Living Root Bridge (Nongriat)
  • 💧 Rainbow Falls — hidden gem
  • 🚗 Drive to Dawki (82 km)
  • 🛶 Boat ride on crystal-clear Umngot River
  • 🌙 Overnight Dawki / Shillong
Day 4 Shillong → Gangtok, Sikkim
  • 🚗 Drive to Bagdogra / NJP (5 hrs)
  • 🚗 Drive to Gangtok (4 hrs)
  • 🏨 Check-in Gangtok hotel
  • 🌆 MG Marg evening walk
  • 🌙 Overnight Gangtok
Day 5 Gangtok Local — Monasteries & Viewpoints
  • 🛕 Rumtek Monastery — largest in Sikkim
  • 🏔️ Tashi Viewpoint — Kanchenjunga sunrise
  • 🌸 Flower Exhibition Centre
  • 🚡 Gangtok Ropeway — aerial valley views
  • 🛍️ Lal Bazaar — local handicrafts
  • 🌙 Overnight Gangtok
Day 6 Yumthang Valley — Valley of Flowers
  • 🚗 Drive to Lachung (119 km)
  • 🌸 Yumthang Valley — rhododendron blooms (seasonal)
  • ❄️ Zero Point — snow at 15,300 ft
  • ♨️ Yumthang hot springs
  • 🌙 Overnight Lachung
Day 7 Return Gangtok → Departure
  • 🚗 Drive back to Gangtok
  • 🚗 Transfer to Bagdogra Airport / NJP Station
  • ✈️ Departure flight
  • 👋 Tour ends — Northeast magic stays forever
