Top Destinations

Must-Visit Places in Ladakh

Explore the most breathtaking locations across the region

Must Visit

Pangong Lake

📍 160 km from Leh

The iconic high-altitude lake that changes colors throughout the day. Crystal blue waters stretching across the Indo-China border at 4,350m.

🕐 Full Day 🌡️ May-Sep ⭐ 4.9/5
Adventure

Nubra Valley

📍 120 km from Leh via Khardung La

A cold desert valley with sand dunes, Bactrian camels, and stunning monasteries. Cross the world's highest motorable pass to reach here.

🕐 2 Days 🌡️ Jun-Sep ⭐ 4.8/5
Spiritual

Thiksey Monastery

📍 19 km from Leh

A stunning 12-story monastery resembling Lhasa's Potala Palace. Home to a 15m tall Maitreya Buddha statue and panoramic valley views.

🕐 2-3 Hours 🌡️ Year Round ⭐ 4.7/5
Scenic

Leh Palace

📍 Leh City Center

A 17th-century royal palace offering breathtaking views of Leh town and the Stok Kangri mountain range. Rich in Ladakhi history and architecture.

🕐 1-2 Hours 🌡️ Year Round ⭐ 4.5/5
Unique

Magnetic Hill

📍 30 km from Leh

A gravity-defying phenomenon where vehicles appear to roll uphill. A fascinating optical illusion on the Leh-Kargil highway.

🕐 30 Min 🌡️ Year Round ⭐ 4.3/5
Serene

Tso Moriri Lake

📍 220 km from Leh

A pristine high-altitude lake at 4,522m, less crowded than Pangong. Home to rare migratory birds and surrounded by snow-capped peaks.

🕐 2 Days 🌡️ Jun-Sep ⭐ 4.8/5

Best Time to Visit Ladakh

Choose your season based on what you want to experience

☀️

Summer

May - September

Peak season with pleasant weather, all roads open, and perfect for sightseeing. Ideal for Pangong, Nubra Valley, and high passes.

15°C - 25°C
❄️

Winter

December - February

Experience the frozen Chadar Trek, snow-covered landscapes, and winter festivals. Only for adventure seekers and cold weather enthusiasts.

-15°C - 5°C
🍂

Autumn

October - November

Fewer tourists, clear skies, and stunning autumn colors. Great for photography but some high passes may close by late November.

5°C - 15°C
🌸

Spring

March - April

Roads start opening, snow begins melting, and wildflowers bloom. Shoulder season with moderate crowds and pleasant temperatures.

5°C - 18°C
