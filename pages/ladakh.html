Top Places in Ladakh

Experience turquoise lakes, ancient monasteries, and vast mountain deserts.

Nature

Pangong Lake

Famous for its ever-changing colours and dramatic setting at 4,350 m. Best visited in summer months.

  • 📍 4–5 hrs drive from Leh
  • 🕐 Best Time: May–Sep
  • 🏃 Duration: Half to full day
Valley

Nubra Valley

Known for sand dunes, double-humped Bactrian camels, and wide open skies near Diskit.

  • 📍 North of Leh via Khardung La
  • 🕐 Best Time: Jun–Sep
  • 🏃 Duration: 1–2 days
Heritage

Leh Palace

Historical palace overlooking Leh; offers insight into Ladakhi history and great viewpoints.

Monastery

Thiksey Monastery

A beautiful monastery with a large seated statue of Maitreya and panoramic views of the Indus valley.

Quirk

Magnetic Hill

A famous gravity-defying stretch of road where vehicles appear to roll uphill — a fun quick stop.
