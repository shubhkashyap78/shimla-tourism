💡 Hotel Booking Tips for Shimla

01

Book Early in Peak Season

May–June and December–January are peak months. Book at least 4–6 weeks in advance.

02

Check Heating Facilities

In winter, make sure your hotel has room heaters or central heating. Nights get very cold!

03

Consider Location

Staying near Mall Road or The Ridge saves transport costs and is convenient for sightseeing.

04

Ask About Parking

If driving to Shimla, confirm parking facilities as vehicle access is restricted in many areas.
