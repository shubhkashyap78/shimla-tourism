Tourist Attractions

Must-Visit Places in Shimla

From ancient temples to colonial landmarks and natural viewpoints, Shimla has something for every kind of traveller.

Landmark ⭐ 4.8

The Ridge

The central hub of Shimla — a large open space in front of Christ Church offering spectacular panoramic views of the Himalayan ranges and the town below.

  • 📍 City Centre, Shimla
  • 🕐 Best Time: Morning & Evening
  • 💰 Entry: Free
  • 🏃 Duration: 1–2 hours
🌟 Must Visit
Shopping ⭐ 4.7

Mall Road

Shimla's most famous promenade — a bustling street lined with Victorian buildings, restaurants, souvenir shops, and cafes. The heartbeat of Shimla.

  • 📍 Main Bazaar, Shimla
  • 🕐 Open: All Day
  • 💰 Entry: Free
  • 🏃 Duration: 2–3 hours
🌟 Must Visit
Temple ⭐ 4.9

Jakhu Temple

One of the oldest temples in Shimla, dedicated to Lord Hanuman. Located at 2,455 m, the temple offers breathtaking 360° views of Shimla and the snow-capped Himalayas.

  • 📍 Jakhu Hill, 2 km from The Ridge
  • 🕐 6:00 AM – 9:00 PM
  • 💰 Entry: ₹20 (Ropeway: ₹250)
  • 🏃 Duration: 2–3 hours
Adventure ⭐ 4.6

Kufri

A popular hill station just 16 km from Shimla, famous for skiing in winter. Enjoy breathtaking Himalayan views, yak rides, and the Himalayan Nature Park with exotic wildlife.

  • 📍 16 km from Shimla
  • 🕐 Best Time: Nov – Feb (snow)
  • 💰 Entry: ₹100
  • 🏃 Duration: 3–4 hours
❄️ Winter Special
Heritage ⭐ 4.7

Christ Church

A magnificent neo-Gothic church built in 1857 — the second oldest church in North India. Famous for its beautiful stained glass windows and colonial architecture.

  • 📍 The Ridge, Shimla
  • 🕐 8:00 AM – 6:00 PM
  • 💰 Entry: Free
  • 🏃 Duration: 1 hour
Heritage ⭐ 4.8

Viceregal Lodge

The grand summer residence of the British Viceroy, now the Indian Institute of Advanced Studies. A stunning example of Elizabethan architecture with manicured gardens.

  • 📍 Observatory Hill, Shimla
  • 🕐 9:30 AM – 5:30 PM (Closed Tue)
  • 💰 Entry: ₹50
  • 🏃 Duration: 2 hours
Nature ⭐ 4.5

Green Valley

A picturesque valley located on the Shimla–Kufri highway, offering stunning views of dense pine and oak forests. A perfect photography and nature walk spot.

  • 📍 On Shimla–Kufri Highway
  • 🕐 Sunrise to Sunset
  • 💰 Entry: Free
  • 🏃 Duration: 1–2 hours
Nature ⭐ 4.4

Chadwick Falls

A stunning 86-metre waterfall located inside a dense forest, flowing at its best during monsoon. A hidden gem that's perfect for nature lovers and trekkers.

  • 📍 Summer Hill, 3 km from Shimla
  • 🕐 Best Time: July – September
  • 💰 Entry: Free
  • 🏃 Duration: 2–3 hours
Heritage ⭐ 4.3

Himachal State Museum

A rich museum showcasing Himachali art, ancient artefacts, miniature paintings, manuscripts, and sculptures — a treasure trove of Himalayan cultural heritage.

  • 📍 Chaura Maidan, Shimla
  • 🕐 10 AM – 5 PM (Closed Mon & Hols)
  • 💰 Entry: ₹25
  • 🏃 Duration: 1.5 hours
