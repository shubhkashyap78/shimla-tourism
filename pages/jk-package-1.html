🗓️ Day-by-Day Itinerary

Day 1 Arrival Srinagar — Dal Lake Shikara & Houseboat
  • ✈️ Arrive Srinagar Airport, meet & greet
  • 🌷 Visit Nishat Bagh & Shalimar Bagh (Mughal Gardens)
  • 🛶 Evening Shikara ride on Dal Lake
  • 🏠 Check-in to traditional houseboat
  • 🍽️ Kashmiri Wazwan dinner on houseboat
  • 🌙 Overnight on Dal Lake houseboat
Day 2 Srinagar Local — Shankaracharya, Hazratbal, Old City
  • 🌅 Sunrise Shikara ride on Dal Lake
  • ⛪ Shankaracharya Temple — panoramic city views
  • 🕌 Hazratbal Shrine — sacred mosque on Dal Lake
  • 🏘️ Old City walk — Jamia Masjid, Nowhatta Bazaar
  • 🛍️ Kashmiri handicrafts & pashmina shopping
  • 🌙 Overnight houseboat
Day 3 Gulmarg — Gondola & Snow Activities
  • 🚗 Drive to Gulmarg (56 km, ~2 hrs)
  • 🚡 Gulmarg Gondola Phase 1 & 2 (world's highest cable car)
  • ❄️ Snow activities — skiing, snowboarding (seasonal)
  • 🏌️ Gulmarg Golf Course — world's highest green
  • 🌄 Sunset views of Nanga Parbat
  • 🌙 Return Srinagar, overnight hotel
Day 4 Pahalgam — Valley of Shepherds
  • 🚗 Drive to Pahalgam (96 km, ~3 hrs)
  • 🌊 Lidder River — trout fishing & riverside walk
  • 🐴 Horse ride to Baisaran (Mini Switzerland)
  • 🎬 Betaab Valley — Bollywood film location
  • 🌿 Aru Valley — pristine meadows
  • 🌙 Overnight Pahalgam hotel
Day 5 Pahalgam → Srinagar — Chandanwari & Sonamarg
  • 🏔️ Morning visit to Chandanwari (Amarnath base)
  • 🚗 Drive back towards Srinagar
  • 🌊 Sonamarg — Glacier views & Sindh River
  • 🏨 Check-in Srinagar hotel
  • 🛍️ Last-minute shopping — saffron, dry fruits, shawls
  • 🌙 Overnight Srinagar
Day 6 Departure from Srinagar
  • 🌅 Morning at leisure
  • 🛶 Optional last Shikara ride
  • ✈️ Transfer to Srinagar Airport
  • 👋 Tour ends — carry Kashmir in your heart
